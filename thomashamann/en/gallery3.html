The Signs of the Zodiac


This is the second series of twelve graphic works with the theme "The Signs of the Zodiac". Three among those are dedicated to the fire, water, earth and air elements. Each one has its own paper format.

"It was a special challenge for me to give form to the inner content of the Signs of the Zodiac as much as possible in a creative way."
